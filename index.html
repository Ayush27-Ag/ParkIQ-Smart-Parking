The flow

From search to gate, four steps.

  1. 01

    Search

    Drop a pin or search any city and see live slot counts before you drive over.

  2. 02

    Reserve

    Pick a time window and lock in the rate. Nothing leaves availability until payment clears.

  3. 03

    Pay

    Checkout runs through Razorpay and every amount is re-verified on our server, not your browser.

  4. 04

    Park

    Get a token at the gate, and pull up directions back to your car whenever you're ready to leave.
